Location Mining in Kadıköy

2017 | İstanbul, Turkey | Bits 'n Bricks

Commisioned by Kadıköy Municipality in Istanbul, we researched multiple alternative data sources to inform urban decision making systems. The aim of the study is to understand the geographic distribution of the purchasing power, rent burden, transpotation access, points of interest and housing market in the neighborhood where official data lacks the resolution.

Visualization and mapping project was precided by an extensive web scrapping phase. At the time of the project Istanbul did not have an open data portal. Data gathering was performed in following fields:

  • Housing: Rental, Sales and short-term rental.
  • Social Media: User demographic determination and POI timeline.
  • Food Delivery
  • POI locations, comments and ratings.

Project was extended to analyze the equitable distribution of resources over the urban landscape. This task was perfomed by combining official and scraped data. Access to local resources was analyzed based on walking distances or approximated coverage area.
