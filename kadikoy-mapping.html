Location Mining in Kadıköy

2017 | İstanbul, Turkey | Bits 'n Bricks

Bits 'n Bricks was commisioned by the Kadıköy Municipality in Istanbul, to researche multiple alternative data sources to inform urban decision making systems following a viral article we published. The aim of the research was to understand the geographic distribution of the purchasing power, rent burden, transpotation access, points of interest and housing market in the neighborhood where official data lacks the resolution.

Visualization and mapping project started by an extensive web scrapping phase. Data gathering was performed in following fields:

  • Housing: Rental, for sale and short-term rental availability.
  • Social Media: User demographic determination and POI timeline.
  • Food Delivery
  • POI locations, comments and ratings.
  • Public transport and facilities.

The project was extended to analyze the equitable distribution of resources over the urban landscape. Access to local resources was analyzed based on walking distances or approximated coverage area.

Although Istanbul Metropolitan Municipality recently announced an Open Data Portal, the progress has been slow and available on this portal has been disappointing for most. To enable mappers and analyst we have decided to publish the data we have been collecting on the city. Some of the data we use in our work be found here.
